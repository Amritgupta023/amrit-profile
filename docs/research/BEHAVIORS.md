# Behaviors

- Pointer spotlight: fixed radial highlight follows cursor over the dark slate background.
- Section navigation: desktop nav links update active state as About, Experience, and Projects pass through the viewport.
- Anchor navigation: nav items use smooth scroll to in-page sections.
- Cards: experience, project, and writing rows gain a translucent slate background on hover; headings turn teal.
- Text links: inline links shift from slate/text color to teal on hover.
- Responsive behavior: at tablet widths the sticky left rail becomes a top intro; at mobile widths row layouts stack and section headings become sticky blurred bars.
